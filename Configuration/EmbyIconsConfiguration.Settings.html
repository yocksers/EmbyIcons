Welcome to EmbyIcons

This plugin overlays icons on your media posters to show audio/video formats, languages, ratings, and more. Start by selecting a profile, assign it to your libraries, then customize which icons to display and where they appear.

Please read the Help section for important information about system requirements and performance.

When disabled, overlays only appear on movies and TV shows.
When enabled, icons will also appear on season posters based on the episodes within that season.

When enabled, episodes in the 'Specials' season won't affect the icons shown on the main series poster.

expand_more Collection Settings

Faster, but less accurate. Only checks the first item to determine icons for the entire collection.

expand_more Filename-based Icons

Display icons for movies based on keywords in the filename (e.g., 'remux', 'extended', 'web-dl'). The icon name you provide is used to find a file like source.icon-name.png in your custom icons folder. Each mapping has its own corner placement, priority, and layout settings.
